About Solomon

My journey in technology began with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Georgia (4.0 GPA, Presidential Scholar). This foundation fueled a career dedicated to unraveling data complexities and harnessing its power for innovation.

With nearly two decades of hands-on experience, from Systems Analyst to a seasoned Data Architect, I've led significant data initiatives at the University System of Georgia and now at the Georgia Data Analytics Center (GDAC). My expertise spans the full data lifecycle: architecting data warehouses, automated ETL pipelines, and user-centric BI solutions.

I thrive on challenges requiring deep technical knowledge and strategic vision—modernizing legacy systems, ensuring data governance, or pioneering new data interaction methods.

Beyond the technical, I'm exploring front-end development and AI/LLMs to revolutionize data interaction, and I'm committed to community building for collaborative learning.
