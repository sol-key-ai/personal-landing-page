The Core Concept

Craft Night is all about active, hands-on creation. It's a space to disconnect from passive consumption and dive into the joy of making. Whether you're soldering a circuit board, knitting a scarf, writing a poem, or coding an app, the focus is on the process of building, learning, and sharing your unique creations and knowledge.

Our Creator Toolkit

We'll provide a basic set of tools and resources to get you started. Specific items may vary per event.

  • Basic Craft Supplies: Paper, scissors, glue, markers.
  • Tech Gear: Wi-Fi, power strips, some microcontrollers (e.g., Arduino, Raspberry Pi - bring your own laptop!).
  • Learning Resources: Access to online tutorials, idea prompts.

Your Creator Kit (What to Bring)

  • Your specific project materials & tools.
  • Your laptop (if working on tech projects).
  • Enthusiasm and an open mind!
  • Optionally, a snack or drink to share.
